# Privacy Policy

**Window Resize** is a macOS menu bar utility that resizes application windows. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Window Resize does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline and makes no network connections.

## Local Storage

All settings (preset sizes, screenshot preferences, launch-at-login) are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your Mac.

## Screenshots

When the optional screenshot feature is enabled, captured images are saved only to a folder you choose on your local file system and/or copied to the system clipboard. Screenshots are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

## Permissions

- **Accessibility:** Required to resize windows of other applications. Managed by macOS System Settings.
- **Screen Recording:** Required for the optional screenshot feature. Managed by macOS System Settings.

These permissions are granted at the operating system level and can be revoked at any time in System Settings > Privacy & Security.

## Third-Party Services

Window Resize does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, advertising, or cloud services.
